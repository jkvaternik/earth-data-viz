CO2 Emissions

Background

CO2 emissions are the main contributor to global warming on Planet Earth, which has several other sideaffects such as melting ice caps, more severe weather, and bleaching of coral reefs. The main source of CO2 comes from the burning of fossil fules. Therefore, in this section we will focus on carbon emissions and energy production for every country contributing to our planetary crisis.

Example Insights

Insight 2

Considerations

For this insight, I will need energy production data for the past 50 years for the countries of focus. Each line will be encoded with color hue in order to differenciate between the different countries. Since CO2 and the energy production data are in different units, I will normalize the data by dividing each data point by the standard deviation of its respective feature. This way, the features may be plotted on the same graph, allowing the user to see if the changes in one line are similar to changes in another line.
